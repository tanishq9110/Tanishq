What is 3D Printing?
Making Real Things
from Digital Ideas.

Imagine drawing something on a computer — and a machine actually builds it in real life, layer by layer, like magic. 🖨️

💻
1. Design It
We create your product on a computer — a lamp, keychain, whatever you want.
🔪
2. Slice It
Software cuts the design into hundreds of thin layers, like slicing bread.
🖨️
3. Print It
The printer melts plastic and builds your item layer by layer from the ground up.
📦
4. Ship It
We inspect, pack it nicely and deliver it straight to your door. Done! 🎉
💡

Fun fact: At 3Dify, every single product is made this way — designed digitally, printed with precision, in your chosen color & material. No factory. Just made for you.

Our Materials
What We Print With

We use only the best filaments so your product looks great, lasts long, and feels premium. 💪

🌿
PLA
Polylactic Acid
Most Popular

Made from corn starch — eco-friendly and biodegradable! Great for detailed prints with a smooth finish.

✔ Eco-friendly & biodegradable
✔ Smooth, clean finish
✔ 30+ color options
⚠ Not ideal for very high heat
🛍 Best for: Lamps, Keychains, Wall Art, Gifts
💪
PETG
Polyethylene Terephthalate
Heavy Duty

The tough guy of filaments. Stronger, more flexible, handles heat better. Like the plastic in water bottles.

✔ Very strong & durable
✔ Heat resistant (up to 80°C)
✔ Slight glossy shine
⚠ Slightly more expensive
🛍 Best for: Desk Organizers, Outdoor Items
✨
Silk PLA
Silky Smooth Finish
Premium

Gives your print a shiny, metallic-looking finish. Perfect when you want something that looks like a gift.

✔ Stunning glossy sheen
✔ Gold, Silver, Rainbow options
✔ Looks like metal, light as plastic
⚠ Less strong than PETG
🛍 Best for: Keychains, Gifts, Decorations
